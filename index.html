Ecuador

Quito, the capital of Ecuador, is located in the Andean region at 2,850 meters above sea level. It is known for its historic center, one of the best-preserved and largest in Latin America and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The city combines colonial architecture with modernity, surrounded by mountains and volcanoes that give it a spectacular landscape. It is also an important cultural, political, and economic center of the country.
